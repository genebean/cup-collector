☕ Cup Collector

Track your Starbucks location cup collection — self-hosted, map-first, real-time sync.

Cup Collector is a Progressive Web App for households that collect Starbucks You Are Here, Been There, and related location-specific cups. It keeps a shared catalog of every cup, shows which ones you own on an interactive map, and finds nearby Starbucks when you're traveling — so you always know whether that cup on the shelf is one you need. It runs entirely on infrastructure you control; no third-party accounts required beyond a Google Places API key.
