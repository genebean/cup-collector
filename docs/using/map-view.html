🗺️ Map View

The map is the default home screen. It gives you a visual overview of the entire collection and nearby Starbucks stores.

Pin colors

Green — a cup you own
Orange — a cup you still need
Blue — a nearby Starbucks store
Pulsing blue dot — your current location

Zooming and navigating

  • Pinch to zoom in and out, or use the + / − buttons.
  • Drag to pan around the map.
  • Tap the 🌍 globe button (top-right of the header) to zoom out to a world view showing your entire collection.
  • When you first open the app, the map automatically zooms to your current location if you've allowed location access.

Tapping pins

  • Tap a cup pin (green or orange) — opens the Cup Detail screen for that cup, showing its image, metadata, and ownership controls.
  • Tap a Starbucks pin (blue) — shows the store name and address, with a "Get directions" link that opens Apple Maps.

Location permission

The app asks for your location when it first opens. If you allow it:

  • The map centers on where you are.
  • A pulsing blue dot marks your position.
  • Nearby Starbucks stores appear as blue pins.
  • The Browse screen sorts unowned nearby cups to the top.

If you deny location permission, the app falls back to a world map view. No broken screens or error messages — the app just works without the location features.

Changed your mind about location?
Go to iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Safari → set to "While Using". Then reload the app.

Nearby Starbucks

When location is active, the app automatically searches for Starbucks stores within about 2km of your position using Google's Places service (server-side — your location is never sent to Google directly). Blue store pins appear on the map. Tap any store pin to see its name, address, and a "Get directions" link that opens Apple Maps with turn-by-turn directions.
