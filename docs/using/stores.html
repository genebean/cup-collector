🏪 Find Stores

The Stores tab is built for trip planning. Search any city and see which Starbucks locations are there — along with what cups might be available at each one.

On this page
  1. Searching for a city
  2. Reading the results
  3. Cups at each store
  4. Getting directions

Searching for a city

Tap Stores in the bottom navigation. Type a city name or address in the search bar and tap Search. The app looks up Starbucks locations in or near that area and shows them as a list. Tap the × inside the search box to clear the input and dismiss the results.

Be as specific or as broad as you like "Nashville, TN", "Paris, France", and "downtown Austin" all work. If results look wrong or sparse, try adding the country or state for a more precise match.

Unlike the map, which centers on your current GPS position, the Stores tab is entirely address-based — no location permission needed and no relation to where you physically are right now. It's designed for planning ahead.

Reading the results

Each store card shows:

  • Store name — typically "Starbucks" with a location identifier
  • Address — full street address
  • Directions → link — opens Apple Maps with turn-by-turn directions to that store
  • Cup summary — a count of needed and owned cups at that location (e.g., "3 needed · 2 owned"). Tap it to expand the full cup list.

If the catalog has no cups associated with a store's area, the card reads "No catalog cups here".

Cups at each store

Tap the cup summary to expand the cups available at that store. Cups are grouped by scope:

  • City cups — any cup whose city centroid is within ~50 miles of the store. Grouped by city name when multiple nearby cities have cups.
  • State cups — any state-scope cup matching the store's state or province
  • Country cups — any country-scope cup matching the store's country

Within each group, needed cups appear first (cups you don't own yet), followed by already owned cups. Tap any cup name to go directly to its detail screen.

Cups follow your collection preferences The Stores tab respects your What I Collect preferences. If you've excluded ornaments or a particular series, they won't appear in store results.

Getting directions

Tap Directions → on any store card to open Apple Maps with walking/driving directions to that Starbucks. This works from anywhere — before the trip, from home, or en route.

If you want to see the store on the map in the context of the surrounding cups, switch to the Map tab and navigate to that city — the store will appear as a blue pin once you zoom into the area and your location is active.
