Getting Started

Welcome to Cup Collector. This guide walks you through installing the app on your phone and logging in for the first time.

Step 1 — Install on your iPhone

Cup Collector is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You don't download it from the App Store — you install it directly from Safari.

  1. Open Safari on your iPhone and go to the app's address: https://cups.yourdomain.com
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up.
  3. Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Tap Add in the top-right corner.
  5. The Cup Collector icon now appears on your home screen. Tap it to open — it runs full-screen, just like a native app.
Use Safari, not Chrome, on iPhone The "Add to Home Screen" PWA installation only works in Safari on iPhone. Chrome and Firefox on iOS cannot install PWAs.

Step 1 (alternate) — Install on Android

Android installs Cup Collector through Chrome:

  1. Open Chrome on your Android device and navigate to the app's address.
  2. Chrome will usually show a banner at the bottom: "Add Cup Collector to Home screen" — tap it.
  3. If no banner appears, tap the three-dot menu (top-right) and choose "Add to Home screen".
  4. Tap Add in the dialog. The icon appears on your home screen.
iOS is the primary supported platform The app works on Android, but it's been designed and tested primarily on iPhone. If something looks off on Android, please report it.

Step 2 — Log in with PocketID

  1. Open the app from your home screen.
  2. Tap "Sign in with PocketID".
  3. You'll be taken to the PocketID login page. Enter your passkey or password.
  4. After logging in you'll be returned to Cup Collector automatically.
Don't have a PocketID account? The household owner needs to create an account for you in PocketID and add it to the correct group. Ask them to follow the PocketID Setup guide.

What you'll see

After logging in the app opens on the Map screen. If you allow location access, the map zooms to your current position and shows:

  • Green pins — cups you already own, placed at the city they represent
  • Orange pins — cups you still need
  • Blue pins — nearby Starbucks stores

The bottom of the screen has four navigation tabs:

  • Map — the default home screen; shows pins for every cup and nearby Starbucks
  • Browse — the full cup catalog with filters; mark cups as owned here
  • Search — instant full-catalog search by city, country, or series
  • Settings — appearance (dark mode), your account info, household switcher, and collection preferences

Your role

Every person in the household has one of two roles:

RoleWhat you can do
Owner Browse the catalog, mark cups as owned, record condition and acquisition info, upload photos, import the cup catalog
Viewer Browse and search the collection — cannot mark cups as owned or make any changes

You can see your role in the Settings tab.

Multiple households

If your account belongs to more than one household — for example, you help maintain a friend's collection — a household switcher appears in the Settings tab. Tap it to switch which collection you're viewing. The change takes effect immediately; no sign-out required.
