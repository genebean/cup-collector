Getting Started

Welcome to Cup Collector. This guide walks you through installing the app on your iPhone and logging in for the first time.

Step 1 — Install on your iPhone

Cup Collector is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You don't download it from the App Store — you install it directly from Safari.

  1. Open Safari on your iPhone and go to the app's address (e.g. https://cups.yourdomain.com).
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up).
  3. Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Tap Add in the top-right corner.
  5. The Cup Collector icon now appears on your home screen. Tap it to open — it will open full-screen, just like a native app.
Use Safari, not Chrome
The "Add to Home Screen" option only works properly in Safari on iPhone. Chrome and other browsers on iOS don't support PWA installation.

Step 2 — Log in with PocketID

  1. Open the app from your home screen.
  2. Tap "Sign in with PocketID".
  3. You'll be taken to the PocketID login page. Enter your username and password.
  4. After logging in, you'll be returned to Cup Collector automatically.
Don't have a PocketID account?
The household owner needs to create an account for you in PocketID and then add your account to the household. Ask them to follow the PocketID Setup guide.

What you'll see

After logging in, the app opens on the Map screen. If you allow location access, the map will zoom to your current location and show:

  • Green pins — cups you already own, placed at the city they represent
  • Orange pins — cups you still need
  • Blue pins — nearby Starbucks stores

The bottom of the screen has four navigation tabs: Map, Browse, Search, and Settings.

Your role

Every person in the household has one of three roles:

RoleWhat you can do
OwnerBrowse, mark cups owned, upload photos, import catalog
CollaboratorSame as owner — full access
ViewerBrowse and view the collection — cannot mark cups as owned

You can see your role in the Settings tab.
