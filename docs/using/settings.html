⚙️ Settings

Tap Settings in the bottom navigation to access your account info, appearance options, and household configuration. What you see here depends on your role.

Account

Shows your name, email address, and role (Owner or Viewer). These are read-only — they come from your PocketID account and can only be changed by your household's PocketID administrator.

Household

Shows the name of your current household (e.g., "Our Collection"). If your account belongs to more than one household, a household switcher appears here instead — tap it to change which collection you're viewing. The switch takes effect immediately; no sign-out required.

Appearance

Choose how the app looks:

  • System — follows your device's light/dark mode setting (default)
  • Light — always light mode
  • Dark — always dark mode

This preference is stored on your device only — it doesn't affect other household members' devices.

App

The App section shows the current app version, a Documentation → link, and a Changelog → link. Documentation opens the in-app docs at /docs — the same content as these pages, served directly from the app. Changelog shows what changed in each release. Both pages include a ← App link in the top bar to return to the app.

What I Collect

Tap "What I Collect" to open the collection preferences screen. This lets you tell the app which types of cups and series your household tracks.

Item types

  • Mugs — always tracked (cannot be turned off)
  • Ornaments — Starbucks also sells miniature ornament versions of some location cups. Toggle this on to include ornaments in your Browse list and map, or off to hide them.

Series

Each cup series (You Are Here, Been There, Discovery Series, etc.) has its own toggle. Turn off any series you don't collect and those cups disappear from Browse and the map — keeping the view focused on what matters to you.

Owned cups always stay visible Turning off a series or item type only hides unowned cups. Any cup you've already marked as owned will still appear in Browse and on the map, regardless of your preferences. You won't lose track of what you have.
Search is not filtered The Search screen always shows every cup in the catalog, regardless of your collection preferences. Use Search to discover cups from series you don't usually track.
Only the household owner can change collection preferences Viewers can open "What I Collect" and see the current settings, but the toggles are disabled. Contact your household owner to change them.

Admin (owners only)

Owners see two links in the Admin section:

  • Import Cups → — opens the in-app cup import tool. Upload a CSV file, preview what will change, and apply the import. For large catalog updates, the CLI script in the dev shell is faster; see Adding Cups.
  • Duplicate Cups → — opens a tool to find and merge duplicate cup records in the catalog.

Sign Out

Tap Sign Out to end your session. You'll be returned to the login screen. Your collection data stays on the server.
